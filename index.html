Bunny breeds

Angora

Angora rabbits are bread for their fur (wool), but it requires substantial attention to keep it clean and mat-free. Great personality is an ideal pet for couples who are ready to expand their family.

Holland Lop

14-years lifespan, weighing only four pounds, and irresistible floppy ears make it the ultimate pet for city dwellers. It’s ideal to give them a specific room to sunbath and hop around.

Mini Lop

They are incredibly adorable, given their small size, lopped ears, and soft coat. Mini lops are known to be some of the most cuddly rabbits, and they enjoy being picked up and pet.

Dutch

They are relatively easy to groom and care for, and they are calm and enjoy being cuddled, though they do best with children who are 10 or older

Lion Head

Great choice for apartment tenants, weighs about three pounds and can live up to 10 years. Families of any size can easily welcome this little lion into their home.

Netherland Dwarf

Small but highly active. Can be stubborn and skittish, meaning they’re not good pets for families or children. Through patience and love, owners soon see their true personality bloom

Rex

They are friendly, athletic, intelligent, and very playful, which — combined with their incredibly soft fur — makes them very popular.

Jersey Wooly

Jersey wooly has a very short body with a chunky head and small, upright ears. Grooming is required to make sure the coat keep clean and free of debris.
